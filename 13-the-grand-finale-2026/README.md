# The Grand Finale 2026

A Pen created on CodePen.

Original URL: [https://codepen.io/ybli-code/pen/gbMwEmb](https://codepen.io/ybli-code/pen/gbMwEmb).

An immersive, cinematic New Year countdown experience built with pure HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

Features a live countdown to 2026, starfield and aurora skies, snow and floating particles, a custom sparkle cursor, interactive fireworks, confetti bursts, neon ring effects, and a synthesized celebration soundtrack with a visualizer.

Click anywhere to launch mini fireworks, or hit Celebrate Now for the full grand finale.
